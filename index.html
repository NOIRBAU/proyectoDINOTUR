About

Zacatlán, Puebla

Its name derives from the Nahuatl words zácatl (grass) and tlan (place suffix) which mean "place where grass abounds". Zacatlán is famous for its old Franciscan Convent and for its watchmaking tradition dating from the early last century, as well as dreamy landscapes shrouded in mist.​​

Be Ecofriendly

  • Don't take anything from nature.
  • Leave your footprints on already established trails.
  • Stay in sustainable accommodations.
  • Minimizes the generation of solid waste.
  • Contribute to the local economy.
  • Participate and honor local traditions.
  • Choose your means of transport well.
